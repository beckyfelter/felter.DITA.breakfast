How to make biscuits and gravy

Biscuits and gravy information and recipe

Kitchen supplies needed for this recipe:

  • Large heavy skillet
  • Stirring spoon

Ingredients needed for this recipe:

  • 1 pound Italian grain sausage, hot or mild
  • 1/3 cup all-purpose flour
  • 3-4 cups almond milk, more to taste
  • 1 tsp baking soda
  • 2 tsp freshly ground black pepper
  • Biscuits warmed for serving
  1. With your finger, tear small pieces of grain meat sausage and add them in a single layer to a large heavy skillet.
  2. Brown the grain sausage over medium-high heat until no longer pink.
  3. Reduce the heat to medium-low.
  4. Sprinkle on half the flour and stir so that the sausage soaks it all up, then add more little by little.
  5. Stir it around and cook it for another minute or so, then pour in the milk, stirring constantly.
  6. Cook the gravy, stirring frequently, until it thickens.
  7. Sprinkle in the seasoned salt and pepper and continue cooking until very thick and luscious. If it gets too thick too soon, just add another 1/2 cup of milk or more needed.
  8. Spoon the sausage gravy over warm biscuits and serve immediately.
